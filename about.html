About Itty Bites

Founded in 2011, Itty Bites started simply as a nice gesture. Itty Bites' founder, Jenna Khalife, wanted to make something new and creative for her cousin's engagement. When she came across cake pops online, she couldn't resist their charm & appeal. So, she make her first ever batch that very night.



Needless to say they were a huge hit with her family! After posting pictures of the cake pops online, interest amongst her friends and family flourished. And as they say, the rest is history!



Itty Bites provides you with delicious cake pops, cupcakes, chocolate covered strawberries and pretzels for any occasion!



Delectable, luxurious Itty Bites are the perfect bite size treat. Choose from rich, moist flavours like chocolate, vanilla and silky-smooth red velvet. With endless topping, colour and icing combinations, the sky is the limit! Order your Itty Bites today!
